You can first use 360 compression to compress PPM, PGM, PBM and other image files, 
and then open the compressed file without decompression to view the above types of image files.
BMP images and JPG images obtained from some functions of this product may not be opened with the photo viewer provided by the operating system.
You can send these images to QQ,In this way, you can see these images directly on QQ, and then save them locally as needed.
